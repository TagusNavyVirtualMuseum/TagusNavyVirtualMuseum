About us

Students from ISEL

This museum was developed as part of the project subject of the Informatics and Multimedia Engineering course at the Instituto Superior de Engenharia de Lisboa (ISEL). We are two finalist students committed to use technology to preserve the traditions of Portugal.



Our Goal

Preservation of portuguese heritage

This project aims to protect the traditional boats of the Tagus River using 3D digital modeling technology. This museum allows anyone in the world to view these vessels and find out more about their history. At the same time this museum prevents these traditional boats from being lost.
